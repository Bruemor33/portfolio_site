What I Do

I am a recent graduate of The Iron Yard's Front-End Engineering program where I most importantly learned how to think like a developer. I really fell in love with the challenge programming offers - the time from when the idea is formed, all the way until completion and deployment. The thing that drew me to programming the most was the ability of developers to identify a problem, outline an approach to solving it, and implementing those plans in a creative way. I am beyond excited to have gained this skillset and look forward to getting out of bed each morning to tackle another problem in an abstract and creative way.
